PDF Anonymization

PDF Anonymimization is designed to anonymize PDF documents in order to remove sensitive content from public distribution.

OCR Anonymization will allow the permanent removal of confidential information – citizens ‘and business partners’ personal data, signatures…, while other information in the document remains machine-readable.

Mark-page Anonymization will allow the permanent removal of confidential information – citizens ‘and business partners’ personal data, signatures…, from scanned documents.

  • Hiding whole words/images with a single click,
  • The ability to “draw” any blocks where the content should be blurred,
  • Easy revision of incorrectly hidden areas.
